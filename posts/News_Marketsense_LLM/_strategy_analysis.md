## Corporate Finance/VC



### Opportunities
*   Anticipated "giga-IPO dreams" from major tech firms like SpaceX, OpenAI, and Anthropic.



### Risks
*   Software stock downturns, crypto market instability, and AI disruption to private equity models.



### Watchlist
*   Monitoring AI-driven market volatility, regulatory scrutiny on M&A, and EV transition strategies.

## Geopolitics



### Opportunities
*   New trade agreements, such as the US-India truce, can foster economic cooperation.



### Risks
*   Geopolitical conflicts, escalating trade wars, and tech sector involvement in international disputes.



### Watchlist
*   Monitoring US trade policy evolution, energy chokepoint vulnerabilities, and foreign tech security risks.

## Macro



### Opportunities
*   Diverse entrepreneurship and regional revitalization are driving US economic acceleration.



### Risks
*   Political interference with the Fed, currency depreciation, and unpredictable antitrust enforcement.



### Watchlist
*   Monitoring Federal Reserve leadership, bond market stability, and labor market health indicators.

## Tech/AI



### Opportunities
*   AI is driving new financial products, healthcare innovation, and manufacturing transformation.



### Risks
*   Overestimated AI productivity, political backlash against AI firms, and potential AI market bubble.



### Watchlist
*   Monitoring AI's impact on employment, smartphone evolution, and key AI developer performance.
